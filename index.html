Privacy Policy - Sample Analysis

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to Sample Analysis, your AI-powered medical morphology diagnostic system. Because we handle medical data, your privacy and data security are our highest priorities. We are committed to protecting the sensitive information of both medical professionals and their patients.

This Privacy Policy outlines:

  • What data we collect and how we use it.
  • How we process medical images and AI diagnostics.
  • How we store and secure your data.
  • Your rights regarding your data.
  • How to contact us for concerns or inquiries.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Professional Account Information

When you register as a medical professional or laboratory technician, we collect:

  • Full name and professional title
  • Email address
  • Secure, hashed passwords

1.2 Patient & Diagnostic Data

To provide our diagnostic services, the app collects and processes:

  • Patient demographic and physical metrics (e.g., Age, Height, Weight, Occupation).
  • Microscopic medical sample images (uploaded securely for AI analysis).
  • AI-generated diagnostic outputs (e.g., Morphology Class, DFI percentage, Motility, and Concentration).
  • Historical ledgers linking unique Sample IDs to specific patient profiles.

1.3 Usage & Diagnostic Data

  • Device information and IP addresses
  • App usage statistics and session durations
  • Crash logs for debugging and improving the AI model

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data strictly for medical and operational purposes:

  • To process uploaded microscopic images using our Convolutional Neural Network (CNN).
  • To generate and store accurate patient diagnostic reports and historical records.
  • To authenticate users and ensure only authorized medical personnel access the database.
  • To prevent database duplication via our dynamic Re-analysis workflows.
  • To reject invalid uploads via our AI Gatekeeper logic.

3. Data Security & Integrity

Because we process health-related data, we employ strict security measures:

  • Data Encryption: All communications between the mobile/web frontend and the Flask backend are secured using HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Relational Integrity: Patient records and historical analyses are stored securely in our relational database (MySQL).
  • File Security: Uploaded images are processed using secure file-handling libraries to prevent malicious server execution.
  • Access Control: Strict role-based authentication ensures data is only accessible to the authorized clinician.

4. Your Rights

As a user, you retain full control over your data. You can:

  • View, update, or append patient records and historical analyses.
  • Request the correction or deletion of specific medical records or images.
  • Delete your professional account entirely.
  • Request a full export of your laboratory's diagnostic history.

5. Third-Party Services & AI Processing

  • AI Processing: Images are processed locally on our secure backend servers using proprietary TensorFlow models. We do not share patient images with public AI training databases.
  • Database Hosting: Data is stored on secure, managed cloud infrastructure complying with standard security protocols.

All third-party infrastructure providers are required to handle your data securely and cannot access unencrypted patient records.
